Tuna Salad

A simple yet flavorful salad made with tender chunks of tuna, fresh juicy tomatoes, and crisp onions. Perfect for a light meal or a quick snack, this combination is both refreshing and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (5 oz or 150g) tuna (in water or oil, drained)
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • Â½ small onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: A squeeze of lemon juice for freshness

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    • Drain the tuna well and transfer it to a mixing bowl
    • Dice the tomato and finely chop the onion
  2. Combine Ingredients
      3. Add the diced tomato and chopped onion to the bowl with the tuna
  3. Add Dressing
    • Drizzle olive oil over the mixture
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional: Add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra freshness
  4. Mix well
    • Gently toss everything together until well combined
  5. Serve
    • Serve immediately as a light meal, on toast, or with crackers
