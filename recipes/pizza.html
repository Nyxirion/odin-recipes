Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish worldwide, originating from Italy. It consists of a flat, round base of dough baked with a variety of toppings, such as tomato sauce, cheese, meats, and vegetables.

Known for its versatility and delicious flavors, pizza can be customized to suit any taste, making it a universal favorite for meals, parties, or quick snacks.

  • 2 ½ cups (300g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp (5g) salt
  • 1 tsp (5g) sugar
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm water
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
    • Mix flour salt and yeast in a bowl
    • Add warm water and olive oil, then knead until smooth
    • Let the dough rise for 1 hour
  2. Make the sauce
    • Combine tomato sauce, garlic powder, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix well
  3. Preheat the oven
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C)
  4. Shape the dough
    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a circle or rectangle
  5. Assemble the pizza
    • Spread the sauce evenly over the dough.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add optional toppings
  6. Bake
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly
  7. Serve
    • Let it cool for 2-3 minutes, slice, and enjoy!
