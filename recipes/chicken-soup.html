Chicken Soup

A comforting and hearty soup made with tender chicken, savory broth, and a blend of fresh vegetables. Perfect for warming up on a cold day or soothing the soul when you need a little comfort.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) chicken (breast, thighs, or a whole chicken)
  • 6 cups (1.5L) chicken broth (or water)
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp dried thyme (or fresh thyme)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Chicken
    • In a large pot, add the chicken and broth (or water). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through
    • Remove the chicken, let it cool, and shred it into bite-sized pieces
  2. Sauté Vegetables
    • In the same pot, sauté onions, garlic, carrots, and celery in a little oil for 5 minutes until softened
  3. Combine and Simmer
    • Add the shredded chicken back to the pot
    • Pour in the broth (if using water, add it now)
    • Add bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper
    • Simmer for 20-25 minutes until the vegetables are tender
  4. Adjust Seasoning
    • Taste and adjust salt and pepper as needed
  5. Serve
    • Remove the bay leaf
    • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot
